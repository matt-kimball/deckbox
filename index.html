Deckbox for Eternal

Deckbox is a widget for displaying decks for the Eternal Card Game in web pages. It is designed to be quickly and easily embedded into existing web sites.

To get started, you'll need to add the deckbox Javascript and CSS to the head of your HTML:

<link rel="stylesheet" href="https://eternal.deckbox.info/deckbox.css"> <script defer src="https://eternal.deckbox.info/deckbox.js"></script>

Then, in the body of your HTML, add a div of class deckbox, and include a decklist. The decklist format is the same format used by Eternal when exporting a deck.

<div class="deckbox" data-deckbox-title="Toshi's Feastcalling Deck" data-deckbox-href= "https://eternalwarcry.com/decks/details/p_L_CIYeBEA/toshis-deck"> 3 Annihilate (Set1 #269) 3 Cauldron Cookbook (Set1004 #2) 4 Dark Return (Set1 #250) 1 End of the Story (Set4 #138) 4 Harsh Rule (Set1 #172) 4 Ixtun Merchant (Set4 #21) 1 Lingering Influence (Set4 #100) 3 Rizahn, Greatbow Master (Set4 #246) 4 Seek Power (Set1 #408) 4 Slay (Set2 #236) 4 Stonepowder Alchemist (Set2 #239) 4 Torch (Set1 #8) 4 Vara, Vengeance-Seeker (Set1004 #19) 2 Vicious Highwayman (Set3 #275) 4 Zuberi, Outlands Warlord (Set4 #37) 3 Crest of Chaos (Set3 #268) 3 Crest of Glory (Set4 #243) 3 Crest of Vengeance (Set3 #264) 2 Fire Sigil (Set1 #1) 2 Justice Sigil (Set1 #126) 3 Seat of Chaos (Set0 #60) 3 Seat of Glory (Set0 #56) 3 Seat of Vengeance (Set0 #55) 2 Shadow Sigil (Set1 #249) 2 Shugo Standard (Set4 #1) --------------MARKET--------------- 1 Bore (Set1003 #1) 1 Cauldron Cookbook (Set1004 #2) 1 Rizahn, Greatbow Master (Set4 #246) 1 Shugo Standard (Set4 #1) 1 Vicious Highwayman (Set3 #275) </div>

That's it!

Deckbox will automatically generate a display with the cards in the deck categorized by function and listed with their rarity and power / influence costs. Each card in the list can be examined by hovering over the card — when mousing over the name of a card, a corresponding image will appear.

data-deckbox-title is used to give the deck a name. data-deckbox-href is optional. If included, it will link the deck title to that location.
